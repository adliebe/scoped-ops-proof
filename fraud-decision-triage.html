What gets delivered

A concise packet your product and engineering team can use.

The work is not a generic memo. It maps how a decision currently moves, where signal gets lost, which evidence needs to survive, and what should be fixed first.

  • Current-state flow map from intake signal to decision, review, escalation, and audit.
  • Evidence-preservation checklist covering raw inputs, derived features, reviewer notes, and customer-visible outcomes.
  • False-positive and false-negative risk points with practical mitigations.
  • Reliability risks around retries, idempotency, queue behavior, logging, exception handling, and replay.
  • Adversarial-input and instruction-conflict abuse cases where document-processing surfaces are involved.
  • Ranked fix list with acceptance criteria, implementation notes, and follow-on milestone options.
